I am a detail-oriented Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Nirma University. I thrive on delivering work with the utmost precision. ## 🚀 Technical Passions & What I'm Building
The intricate design of modern hardware inspires me to push boundaries. My core focus lies at the intersection of hardware architecture and machine learning:

* **Edge AI & Machine Learning:** Researching TinyML, Few-Shot Learning, and optimizing YOLO models for edge devices and high-speed ASIC deployment.
* **Hardware Design & Co-Design:** Innovating with FPGA/ASIC devices. I am currently engineering a fully custom CVA6 (Ariane) RISC-V 64-bit SoC integrated with NVIDIA's NVDLA on Xilinx FPGAs, as well as building Over-The-Air (OTA) FPGA reconfiguration pipelines.
* **Hardware Security:** Exploring cryptography, side-channel attack pipelines (TVLA/CPA), and Physical Unclonable Functions (PUF) for secure device communication.
* **Photonic Computing:** Exploring how light-based processing will revolutionize hardware acceleration and unlock next-generation computational speeds.
